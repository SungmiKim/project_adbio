A BETTER FUTURE

As the world’s only leading developer of immune antibodies for livestock, fisheries,
and the human body, ADBIOTECH Co., Ltd., is developing various products that can replace vaccines
and antibiotics based on technological know-how accumulated over the past 16 years.

Immune antibody production technology

Based on the antibody production technology using eggs and nanobodies, we have developed a variety of products to prevent and treat diseases in livestock, fisheries, and humans that replace vaccines and antibiotics.

Various pipelines

We have developed antibody products for IgY fisheries such as salmon, shrimp, and eel at the request of foreign countries and continue research by applying them to various fields such as human helico IgY and cholesterol IgY.

Realizing sales

We are an R&D and manufacturing company that leads livestock IgY products to sales in Korea and overseas and sells the newly developed fishery IgY products, engaging in all stages: Research → Development → Production → Sales
